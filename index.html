Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

A complete outreach stack — campaigns, sequences, pipeline, and analytics — built directly into Google Sheets.

📧

Personalized campaigns

Send hundreds of handcrafted emails from Gmail. Template variables auto-fill first name, company, meeting link, and more. Every email looks one-to-one.

👁

Open & reply tracking

See who opened your email and how many times. Zentry auto-detects replies by scanning your inbox — no forwarding or plugins needed.

🔁

Automatic follow-up sequences

Set up Day 3, Day 7, Day 14 follow-ups in seconds. Sequences stop automatically when a contact replies. Set it and forget it.

📊

Sales Channel Kanban

Drag contacts through custom stages — Lead, Contacted, Meeting, Proposal, Closed Won. Color-coded by campaign so you always know where deals came from.

💬

One-click SMS

Click any phone number to open Messages with a pre-filled text. Personalized with first name. Ready to send in one tap.

📅

Meeting scheduling

Create Google Calendar events with Meet links directly from the contact card. Stage auto-updates to Meeting. No tab switching.

🚫

Unsubscribe management

One-click unsubscribe in every email. Zentry automatically skips opted-out contacts forever. Stay compliant without thinking about it.

📈

Dashboard & analytics

Track open rates, reply rates, pipeline value, and win rate. Filter by This Year, Last Year, or All Time. See every campaign side by side.

🔍

Duplicate detection

Instantly scan your entire contact database for duplicate names, emails, and phone numbers across all campaigns at once.

How it works

Up and running in 2 minutes.

No complex onboarding. No new tools. ZentryCRM lives inside Google Sheets — the tool you already use every day.

1

Install from Marketplace

One click install from Google Workspace Marketplace. No signup, no credit card. Opens in any Google Sheet.

2

Create a campaign

Name your campaign and ZentryCRM builds the sheet instantly — all columns ready, all tracking set up.

3

Add contacts & preview

Paste contacts or import from any sheet. Preview exactly what each person receives before sending.

4

Send the campaign. Follow-ups schedule automatically. Replies are detected. Your dashboard updates in real time.
