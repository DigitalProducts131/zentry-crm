Features

Everything you need to close more deals

Zentry gives sales reps a full outreach stack — without leaving Google Sheets.

📧

Personalized email campaigns

Send hundreds of personalized emails directly from Gmail. Each one looks hand-written — because it is.

👁

Open & reply tracking

See who opened your email and how many times. Zentry automatically scans your Gmail inbox to detect replies.

💬

One-click SMS

Click any phone number to open your Messages app pre-filled with a personalized text — ready to send in seconds.

📊

Pipeline tracking

Track every lead from first contact to closed deal. See your entire pipeline at a glance inside your sheet.

🚫

Unsubscribe management

One-click unsubscribe in every email. Zentry automatically skips opted-out contacts on future campaigns.

🔍

Duplicate detection

Instantly scan your entire contact database for duplicate names, emails, and phone numbers across all sheets.

How it works

Up and running in 2 minutes

No complex setup. No new tools. Zentry lives right inside Google Sheets.

1

Install from Marketplace

Find Zentry on the Google Workspace Marketplace and install it with one click. No credit card required.

2

Add your contacts

Paste your contact list into any Google Sheet. Zentry finds the right columns automatically.

3

Write your email

Use one of our proven templates or write your own. Preview exactly what each contact will receive.

4

Send & track

Hit send and watch your Campaign Tracker update in real-time with opens, replies, and pipeline status.
